What is Qwan Ki Do ?

Qwan Ki Do is not easily defined: A Martial Art is not merely practiced, it is lived... For some it is physical activity,self- discipline ,Click here for more... or a competitive sport. For others, it is a means of better self-knowledge, understanding and mastery: truly a way of living. The Qwan Ki Do is all of that together and none of that separately. Qwan Ki Do is a discipline which requires total dedication to practice, to mental and physical development. It is one of the greatest methods of Vietnamese Martial Arts. In conclusion, the Qwan Ki Do is : A Martial Art method Martial art personality A Martial Art authority for the world, the World Union Of Qwan Ki Do. World Organisations

Successful 5th Qwan Ki Do children and European youth championship in Zurich: Results of the competition The weekend of 27.-28. April saw the 5th Qwan Ki Do Children's and Youth European Championships at Saalsporthalle, Zurich. Qwan Ki Do is a Vietnamese martial art. The Swiss Qwan Ki Do Association organized this championship and even participated with a 55-strong delegation of children, youth and judges. A total of 465 athletes from 62 clubs participated in the "Quyen" category and 317 athletes from 60 clubs in the "Combat" category. The participating children and teenagers, aged seven to seventeen, came from eleven different European countries.
